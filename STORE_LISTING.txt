Full description:
---------------------------------------------------------------
Tapstone is a shading logic puzzle carved in slate and chalk.

Shade cells to form a single connected shape — no 2×2 block allowed
anywhere. Clue cells stay unshaded and show one to three numbers: reading
that cell's neighbors clockwise, the numbers are the lengths of the shaded
runs touching it. Work outward from what the numbers rule out, and the whole
shape comes into focus.

There is no guessing. Each of the 150 puzzles has been checked by a real
solver to guarantee a single, logical shape — a path of pure deduction
always exists from the first cell to the last.

FEATURES
• 150 hand-verified puzzles across three tablets
   – Slate (6×6), Tablet (7×7), Monolith (8×8)
• Guaranteed unique solution on every board — solvable by logic alone
• Simple tap controls: cycle a cell between shaded, X, and blank
• Live 2×2 violation highlighting so mistakes are easy to catch and fix
• Earn up to three stars per puzzle for efficient solving
• Cool slate-and-chalk visuals with cyan clue numerals
• Calm ambient soundtrack and tactile feedback — both toggleable
• Fully offline. No ads, no accounts, no tracking. Progress stays on your
  device.

Shade the one true shape.
