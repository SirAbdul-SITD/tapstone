Privacy Policy — Tapstone

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Tapstone is a single-player logic puzzle game. We respect your privacy and collect nothing.

Information We Collect

None. Tapstone does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics.

Local Storage

Your puzzle progress and settings (solved levels, stars, and your sound and haptics preferences) are stored only on your own device. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you uninstall the app.

Third Parties

Tapstone does not use third-party advertising, tracking, or analytics services.

Children

Tapstone is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published here with a new date.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, please use the contact details on the app's store listing.
